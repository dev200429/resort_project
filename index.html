Welcome to Morgah Resorts

Discover Serenity in our gated resort in Rawalpindi.


Nestled in the heart of Rawalpindi, just a short walk away from the scenic Ayub Park, our facilities offer the perfect retreat for those seeking peace and relaxation. Surrounded by lush greenery stretching as far as the eye can see, our resort provides an idyllic escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Designed with a nod to colonial architecture, our resort stands out with its timeless charm and elegance. The thoughtfully crafted interiors of our tastefully furnished guest rooms provide a space where you can truly relax and unwind. In addition to our serene accommodations, we offer spacious, versatile event halls that are ideal for weddings, birthdays, corporate gatherings, and other special occasions. Whether you are hosting a family celebration or a grand event, our dedicated staff will work with you to create an unforgettable experience amidst beautiful surroundings. Whether you are here for a weekend getaway, a family vacation, or a special event, our resort provides the perfect backdrop to “relax, unwind, and leave the world behind.”
